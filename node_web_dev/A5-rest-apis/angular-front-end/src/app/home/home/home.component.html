Viewing the team collection

  • Click on the user icon/dropdown menu in the top right
  • The Team collection (teams that the logged in user has access to, could be 0) will be listed in between the 'Your Profile' and 'New Team' selections

Creating a team

  • Click on the user icon/dropdown menu in the top right
  • Click 'New Team'
  • Complete the wizard, adding a name (don't end your name in a space), description, and email
  • After the wizard it completed youll be directed to that teams page
  • The team has been created, notice if you go to the home page it now shows up in the navbar dropdown and you can click it to view it

Modifying a Team

  • Click on the user icon/dropdown menu in the top right
  • Click on one of teams in the dropdown (youll be sent ot that teams settings page)
  • Change any of the settings you want and click save changes. Notice on refresh, that the changes have persisted

Deleting a Team

  • Click on the user icon/dropdown menu in the top right
  • Click on one of teams in the dropdown (youll be sent ot that teams settings page)
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page a click 'Delete this team'
  • Confirm the teams name to enable the delete button then press delete and press the green success button to return home
  • Notice the team has been deleted and is not longer in the dropdown menu or accessible via /teams/:teamId
